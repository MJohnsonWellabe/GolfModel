# Development Environment Roadmap

## Preserve Play Space
- Preserve environmental assets within ~20 yards of fairways, greens, tees, bunkers, and intended recovery areas.
- Don't thin vegetation that creates the feeling of playing in the woods or natural landscape.

## Bounded World
- Stop detailed rendering shortly beyond the playable corridor.
- Fade into fog, haze, or distant silhouettes.
- Avoid giant cliffs just to hide map edges.
- The world should feel compact and intentional.

## Connected Course
- Whenever possible, use neighboring holes beyond the render boundary instead of empty terrain.
- Players should occasionally see adjacent holes, making the course feel like one connected property.
