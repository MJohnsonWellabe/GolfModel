Courses with a sense of place.

Each course is hand-authored — its own terrain, hazards, planting and light.

Wildwood Glen

A lush parkland course — cherry-blossom groves, still ponds and tight, tree-lined fairways that reward shaping the ball into the pin.

Sable Bay

Port Johnson Links

Scottish links through and through — deep pot bunkers, firm, fast-running turf and a tide that has the final say on the day.

Depth under the pick-up-and-play

Real shot-making, one thumb.

Systems a real golfer will feel — and a newcomer can learn in a hole or two.

Spin & shot shaping

Shape it, spin it, hold it.

Dial in draw or fade and set your spin — top for run-out, back to check and bite. The ball flies, lands and reacts the way you struck it, so every green is a target you can actually attack.

True Vision

Read the break before you commit.

Spend a True Vision charge to reveal the full predicted line — carry, curve and the roll of a big green — then trust it or play the hero line. Every round starts with a free charge.

Fire streaks

String together perfect swings, catch fire.

Two all-perfect swings in a row and you're on fire — a temporary boost to your stats that lasts until you miss one. Momentum you can actually feel, hole to hole.

Earned, not just bought

A store and a season pass, both worth playing for.

Cosmetics and club upgrades you earn by playing — real money only ever buys a coin top-up or the pass itself, never a shortcut past it.

The store

Ball tints, trails, outfits, club skins — and characters.

Spend J-Coins earned on the course for cosmetic flair and modest club upgrades across driver, irons, wedges and putter. Five characters are free from the start; the rest are store unlocks.

Season One

50 levels, marquee pals at the top.

Play rounds, climb the pass, and claim a run of exclusive companion pals along the way — with the season's showpiece finale waiting at level 50.
