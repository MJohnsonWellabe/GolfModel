Spin & shot shaping

Shape it, spin it, hold it.

Dial in draw or fade and set your spin — top for run-out, back to check and bite. The ball flies, lands and reacts the way you struck it, so every green is a target you can actually attack.

True Vision

See the whole line before you commit.

Spend a True Vision charge to reveal the full predicted trajectory — carry, curve and roll — then trust it or play the hero line. Every round starts with a free charge.

Tree occlusion

The camera never loses your golfer.

Tucked in the trees? Any canopy between you and the camera softly fades to a ghost, so you always see the ball, the lie and the swing — no fighting the view out of the woods.
